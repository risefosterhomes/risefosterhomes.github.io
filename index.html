When a child enters the foster care system, what comes after? Statistically, around seventy five percent of these children will leave and are susceptible to the predators of human trafficking. Our goal is to afford several homes that would allow a transitional process that prepares children to become independent and functional members of our society, but most importantly, provide a family to always come home to.

As of today, twenty seven children have gone through our program. Just imagine how God could multiply this number with your help. However, this care is expensive and cannot be done without you. Please consider clicking the donation link and assisting a child with a Christ-centered home, food, clothing, traveling expenses, school supplies and even a chance to search for their biological parents.

May God bless you for your efforts, and always remember that children should be saved, not sold
